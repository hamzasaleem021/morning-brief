Privacy Policy

Your Data, Your Call

What we collect, why, and how you can opt out.

The short version

Morning Brief is a personal intelligence tool. We store only what's needed to make it work across your devices — nothing more. No behavioral tracking, no ads, no data sales.

This policy tells you exactly what's stored, where, and how to delete it.

What we store

On your device (always):

  • Your list of RSS sources (name, URL, display order, color assignment)
  • Your theme preference (light, dark, or auto)
  • Session counts used to decide when to suggest installing the app to your home screen

These live only in your browser's localStorage. Clearing your browser data removes them.

In the cloud (only if you sign in):

  • Your email address (to send you the sign-in link)
  • Your source list (so it can appear on your other devices)

That's it. We don't store what you read, when you read it, or which articles you opened.

What we don't collect

  • Reading history. Articles you open are tracked by your browser, not by us.
  • Click data. When you click through to an article, your browser handles it directly. We don't proxy, log, or measure it.
  • Behavioral analytics. No Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no Mixpanel. This build of Morning Brief includes no third-party tracking whatsoever.
  • IP address logs. We don't keep access logs beyond what Supabase requires for security.
  • Cookies for tracking. We use a single cookie for your authentication session when signed in. That's all.

The RSS proxy

RSS feeds are fetched through a Cloudflare Worker to handle CORS. The proxy:

  • Sees the URL of the RSS feed you're fetching
  • Does not log which user requested which feed
  • Does not store feed contents
  • Does not modify responses

Your IP address is visible to Cloudflare during the request, as with any web request.

Authentication

Sign-in uses Supabase magic links. There's no password to manage — we email you a one-time link. Your email is stored in Supabase's auth.users table, and a session cookie is set when you're signed in.

You can sign out at any time from within the app, which removes the session cookie. To delete your account entirely, reach out and we'll remove your row from the database.

Your rights

  • Use without signing in. All core functionality works without an account. Sync is optional.
  • Delete your local data. Clear your browser storage, or use the "Remove" button on each source.
  • Delete your synced data. Sign out to stop syncing, or contact us to remove your cloud record entirely.
  • Export your sources. Your source list lives in localStorage as JSON. You can view and export it directly from your browser's DevTools.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change what we store or how — for example, by adding analytics — we will clearly announce it in-app and in the CHANGELOG before any change takes effect. Every shipped version of Morning Brief preserves the promise on this page.
